----------------------------------SMB Enumeration---------------------------------------
Ports: 139, 445

#Built in NMAP script to enumerate SMB shares

nmap -p 445 --script=smb-enum-shares.nse,smb-enum-users.nse 10.10.168.22

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

#Use "smbclient" to connect to shares

smbclient //<ip>/<user>

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

#Use "smbget" to recursively download the share

smbget -R smb://<ip>/<user>
